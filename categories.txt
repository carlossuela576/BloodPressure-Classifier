Normal:
Systolic: Less than 120
Diastolic: Less than 80

Elevated:
Systolic: 120-129
Diastolic: Less than 80

Hypertension Stage 1:
Systolic: 130-139
Diastolic: 80-89

Hypertension Stage 2:
Systolic: 140 or higher
Diastolic: 90 or higher

Hypertensive Crisis:
Systolic: Higher than 180
Diastolic: Higher than 120
